Cookie Policy

Welcome to our Cookie Policy. Ensuring the privacy of your data is important to us, and this policy explains how we use cookies in relation to our financial planning training platform.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies on our Website and mobile application (collectively referred to as the "Platform"). By using our Platform, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. Cookies help us to provide a better experience while using our financial planning training services.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the Platform to remember your preferences and activities to enhance your experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies (which are deleted once you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted). These cookies help us improve your experience by remembering your actions and preferences.

4. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used to:

  • Improve the functionality of the financial planning training platform.
  • Provide personalized learning experiences based on your preferences.
  • Analyze how users interact with our Platform to enhance our services.

5. Controlling and Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may impact your ability to access certain features of our Platform.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services, such as analytics tools, that place cookies on your device. These cookies help us understand how users engage with our Platform and improve our financial training services.

7. Security of Your Data

We take appropriate measures to ensure that any data collected via cookies is stored securely. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in how we use cookies. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about any updates.
