Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Protecting your privacy is important to us.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, protect, and use personal data ("Personal Data") that you ("User", "You", or "Your") provide through the financial planning training platform website and mobile application (collectively referred to as the "Website" or "App"). It also outlines your choices regarding your personal data and how you can access and update it. This Policy does not apply to the practices of third-party services we do not own or control.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and store any information you knowingly provide when registering on the Website or App, subscribing to updates, or enrolling in training programs. You may choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your access to specific features of the Website or App.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you access the Website or App, our servers automatically log certain data sent by your browser or device. This may include your IP address, device type, operating system, browser version, language preference, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.

4. Use of Collected Information

We use the collected data to enhance user experience, improve customer service, and optimize the financial planning training platform. Non-personal data is analyzed to identify trends and improve the performance of the Platform. This data is not used in a way that personally identifies any user.

5. Cookies

The financial planning training Website and App may use cookies to store user preferences and collect analytics. You can control cookie settings in your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some functionalities of the Website.

6. Advertisements and Analytics

Our Website and App may display advertisements based on user activity. We may use third-party services for analytics to understand user behavior and improve performance. You can opt out of personalized ads through the settings of your browser or device.

7. Security of Your Data

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Links to Other Websites

The financial planning training Website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies when visiting external websites.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services. We encourage users to review this page for the latest updates.
